InTune

SLIC Team

Microsoft Cognitive Services + Music

Hello, we are team SLIC, standing for Sonya, Laurissa, Ian, and Christin. Sonya, Christin, and Ian are all three students from Newport High School in Bellevue, Washington while Laurissa is a computer science major at the University of Washington in Seattle. We came together to create our project called "inTune" where we measure the emotions of people and play the type of music corresponding from the outcome of it. We alter with the Microsoft Cognitive Services API and connect with the logarithmic function we created from music notes. We are thankful to have this opportunity to participate in the 2017 Def Hackathon at Microsoft this year!
