InTune

SLIC Team

Microsoft Cognitive Services + Music

Hello, we are team SLIC, standing for Sonya, Laurissa, Ian, and Christin. Sonya, Christin, and Ian are all students from Newport High School in Bellevue, Washington while Laurissa is a computer science major at the University of Washington in Seattle. We came together to create our project called "inTune" where we capture the mood of people and play a corresponding short musical excerpt. We utilized the Microsoft Cognitive Services API and connect an algorithm organizing chord progressions. We are thankful to have this opportunity to participate in the 2017 Def Hackathon at Microsoft this year! Our goal is to represent people's feeling in the form of music.
